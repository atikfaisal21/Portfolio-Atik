Welcome

Exploring Materials Science Through Innovation and Research

I am a final-year undergraduate student at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), passionate about advancing materials science through computational methods and experimental research. My work combines theoretical understanding with practical laboratory experience to solve real-world industrial challenges.

About Me

With a strong foundation in materials characterization, simulation techniques, and computational materials science, I am committed to pushing the boundaries of materials engineering. My research interests span composite materials, thin film deposition, metallurgical defects analysis, and optoelectronic material design.

I leverage cutting-edge tools like DFT simulations, machine learning models, and advanced characterization techniques (SEM, UV-Vis spectroscopy, XRD) to predict material properties and discover novel materials for industrial applications.

Currently preparing PhD applications in the USA, I am eager to contribute to the field through groundbreaking research and innovative solutions to global materials challenges.
